About

The Never Ending Degen Dream is a 666-supply NFT collection created by Boplord (Jim Björklund), a Stockholm-based artist and music producer. Born during the 2021-2022 Web3 wave, the project was a creative response to the hype-driven, copy-paste culture of early NFT drops — designed instead as an authentic experiment in community, humor, and self-expression.

Built live over eight days within the Mutant Shiba Club community, the collection helped fund Boplord's music production studies while establishing his footprint as a multidisciplinary creative. The project deliberately avoided a strict roadmap, embracing the chaos and spontaneity of degen culture under the guiding motto: “The Never Ending Degen Dream.”

Early concepts included a platformer game called Bopiverse, an in-house merch line with embroidered apparel, and musical integration through releases under Boplord and Bopgang3000. While those features were never fully realized, the project achieved its true aim — to stand as a statement of creative independence in a speculative era of Web3.

If it made you laugh, think, or feel something genuine, then it served its purpose.
— Boplord
